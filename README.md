## **Blinkit Grocery Dashboard**

### Overview
The Blinkit Grocery Dashboard provides a consolidated view of sales, customer ratings, and product performance across different outlet types and locations. It highlights total and average sales, item categories, outlet size, and regional trends. The dashboard helps management monitor performance, identify high-performing outlets, and make data-driven decisions to improve efficiency and growth.

---

### Objective
The objective of the Blinkit Grocery Dashboard is to analyze sales performance, outlet efficiency, and product demand across different locations and outlet types. It helps identify trends, top-performing categories, and areas for improvement to support data-driven business decisions.

### Key Insights

- Total sales reached 1.20M with an average sale value of 141 and an average rating of 3.9.

- Supermarket Type 1 contributes the highest sales (788K), making it the top-performing outlet type.

- Tier 3 locations generate the highest sales (472.1K), followed by Tier 2 and Tier 1.

- Medium-sized outlets perform better compared to small and high outlets.

- Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods are the top-selling item categories.
